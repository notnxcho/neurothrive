About Us
Organization Overview
Neuro Thrive Foundation was founded to address the unique
wellness barriers facing neurodivergent individuals, especially
teens and young adults who often lose services as they age. Our
inclusive approach blends adaptive fitness, nutrition coaching,
and independent living skills in a nurturing environment, fostering
confidence and lifelong growth.
Vision: Full access to wellness, meaningful social connection, and
opportunity for all.
Mission &amp; Values
Mission: To advance the health, wellness, and independent living
skills of neurodivergent youth and adults—including those with
autism spectrum disorder and related conditions—using

evidence-based fitness, nutrition, and behavior-focused
education. We deliver direct services, specialized caregiver and
professional training, and inclusive community wellness initiatives
to bridge health disparities and promote equal participation.
Values: Inclusion, Evidence-Based Practice, Empowerment,
Health Equity, Community.

Programs &amp; Services
Adaptive Fitness Programs
 Small group classes and1:1 coaching.
 Goal areas: Strength, coordination, self-regulation, and
independence for neurodivergent teens and adults.
Nutrition &amp; Healthy Living
 Interactive sessions in meal planning, safe cooking, and
nutrition education tailored to neurodivergent learning styles.
Wellness Education
 Connect movement and executive functioning; workshops
for sleep, hygiene, and time management; caregiver and
professional training for parents, ABA teams, and community
providers; ACT-informed wellness coaching.
Community Wellness Initiatives

 Inclusive outings, seasonal camps, weekend “Healthy Habits
Club,” and public events with local partners.​

For Families &amp; Caregivers
